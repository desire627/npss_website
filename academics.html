Our Academic Philosophy

At Nyahuka Parents' Secondary School, we provide a balanced education that combines academic rigor with character development. Our curriculum is designed to challenge students intellectually while fostering creativity, critical thinking, and a lifelong love of learning.

Academic Programs

Ordinary Level (O-Level)

Our 4-year O-Level program covers core subjects including:

  • Mathematics
  • English Language
  • Integrated Science
  • Social Studies
  • Local Language
  • Elective subjects

Advanced Level (A-Level)

Our 2-year A-Level program offers specialization in:

  • Sciences (Physics, Chemistry, Biology)
  • Arts (History, Geography, Literature)
  • Business (Economics, Entrepreneurship)
  • Mathematics

Vocational Training

Practical skills development programs:

  • Computer Studies
  • Agriculture
  • Home Economics
  • Woodworking
  • Metalwork
