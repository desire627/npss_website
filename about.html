Our History

Nyahuka Parents' Senior Secondary School was founded in 2001 by a dedicated group of parents who envisioned a future where every child in the Nyahuka region could access quality secondary education.

Our Mission

To provide holistic education that nurtures academic excellence, discipline, and strong moral values through collaboration between parents, educators, and the community.

Our Vision

To be a center of excellence in rural education by empowering future leaders through quality, accessible, and value-based learning.

As the founder (CEO) of Nyahuka Parents' Senior Secondary School, I am driven by a deep commitment to transforming education into a powerful tool for community upliftment. My vision has always been to create a nurturing environment where students from all backgrounds can unlock their potential, not just academically but also morally and socially. I believe that quality education should be accessible, values-based, and relevant to the challenges of our times. Through this school, I aim to cultivate critical thinkers, responsible citizens, and future leaders who are grounded in integrity and cultural identity. Establishing this institution has taught me that true impact lies not just in infrastructure, but in empowering passionate educators, involving parents, and consistently aligning our efforts with the needs of the learners and the wider community.
